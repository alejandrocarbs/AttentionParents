The Signs of Bullying!

A child rarely may be open about being teased at school. However, there are a number of signs to look out for. Its going to manifest itself by your child saying, "I don't want to go to school today."
Unexplainable injuries.
Changes in eating habits.
Lost or destroyed clothing, electronics, jewlery.
Feeling of helplessness.
Decreased self esteem.
